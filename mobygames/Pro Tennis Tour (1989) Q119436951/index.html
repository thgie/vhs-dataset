Official Description (Ad Blurb)

THE ULTIMATE TENNIS SIMULATION

Okay Tennis Ace, now's your chance to compete with the best. You've always wanted to test your skills against top-seeded players. Pro Tennis Tour - The Ultimate Tennis Simulation ranks you 64th amongst the best tennis players in the world.

Set your sights on such championships as the Australian Open, Roland Garros, Wimbledon, and the US Open.

Step into centre court, tighten your grip and prepare to serve up your best shot - Pro Tennis Tour is about to begin.

Source: Back of Box - Amiga (UK) - Hit Squad release
